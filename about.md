<div class="week-header">
  <h1>Methodology</h1>
  <p style="color: var(--gray); margin-top: 1rem;">How developments are selected, analyzed, and presented in this tracker</p>
</div>

<section>
  <h2>Selection Process</h2>
  
  <div class="card">
    <h3>Daily Review Workflow</h3>
    <p style="color: var(--gray); line-height: 1.8;">
      <strong>9:00 AM IST Daily:</strong>
    </p>
    <ol style="margin-left: 1.5rem; color: var(--gray); line-height: 1.8; margin-top: 1rem;">
      <li>Monitor 30+ authoritative sources (regulatory bodies, legal publishers, major news outlets)</li>
      <li>Identify developments published in last 24 hours</li>
      <li>Apply selection criteria to filter signal from noise</li>
      <li>Research context and verify facts</li>
      <li>Draft analysis and publish entries</li>
    </ol>
    <p style="color: var(--gray); margin-top: 1rem;">
      <strong>Volume:</strong> Typically 3-7 entries per day from 50-100+ developments reviewed.
    </p>
  </div>
</section>

<section>
  <h2>Selection Criteria</h2>
  
  <p style="color: var(--gray); margin-bottom: 2rem;">A development makes the tracker if it meets at least one of these criteria:</p>
  
  <div class="grid grid-2">
    <div class="card">
      <h3 style="font-size: 1.2rem; color: var(--primary);">1. Regulatory Impact</h3>
      <ul style="color: var(--gray); line-height: 1.8; margin-left: 1.2rem;">
        <li>New legislation, regulations, or binding guidance</li>
        <li>Court decisions setting precedent</li>
        <li>Enforcement actions revealing priorities</li>
        <li>Changes to compliance frameworks</li>
      </ul>
      <p style="color: var(--gray); margin-top: 1rem; font-style: italic;">
        Examples: EU AI Act deadlines, FTC enforcement, landmark rulings
      </p>
    </div>
    
    <div class="card">
      <h3 style="font-size: 1.2rem; color: var(--primary);">2. Market Significance</h3>
      <ul style="color: var(--gray); line-height: 1.8; margin-left: 1.2rem;">
        <li>Major corporate policy changes</li>
        <li>Industry consolidation or market exits</li>
        <li>Products with novel legal implications</li>
        <li>Funding decisions revealing trends</li>
      </ul>
      <p style="color: var(--gray); margin-top: 1rem; font-style: italic;">
        Examples: Robin AI collapse, M&A in legal tech, platform policy changes
      </p>
    </div>
    
    <div class="card">
      <h3 style="font-size: 1.2rem; color: var(--primary);">3. Cross-Border Relevance</h3>
      <ul style="color: var(--gray); line-height: 1.8; margin-left: 1.2rem;">
        <li>Developments influencing multiple jurisdictions</li>
        <li>International cooperation or standards</li>
        <li>Extraterritorial application of laws</li>
      </ul>
      <p style="color: var(--gray); margin-top: 1rem; font-style: italic;">
        Examples: US federal preemption (influences EU), OECD AI principles
      </p>
    </div>
    
    <div class="card">
      <h3 style="font-size: 1.2rem; color: var(--primary);">4. Precedent-Setting</h3>
      <ul style="color: var(--gray); line-height: 1.8; margin-left: 1.2rem;">
        <li>First-of-its-kind regulatory action</li>
        <li>Novel legal arguments</li>
        <li>Emerging regulatory approaches</li>
      </ul>
      <p style="color: var(--gray); margin-top: 1rem; font-style: italic;">
        Examples: First AI safety enforcement, novel application of existing law
      </p>
    </div>
  </div>
</section>

<section>
  <h2>What Gets Excluded</h2>
  
  <div class="info-box" style="background: #fee; border-left-color: #ef4444;">
    <h4 style="color: #dc2626;">Never Included</h4>
    <ul style="color: var(--gray);">
      <li><strong>Commentary-only articles</strong> - Opinion pieces without new factual developments</li>
      <li><strong>Duplicate coverage</strong> - Multiple outlets reporting same development</li>
      <li><strong>Marketing disguised as news</strong> - Vendor announcements without regulatory significance</li>
      <li><strong>Speculation</strong> - Predictions without concrete basis</li>
      <li><strong>Incremental updates</strong> - Minor procedural steps in known processes</li>
      <li><strong>Regional laws with limited scope</strong> - Unless precedent-setting</li>
    </ul>
  </div>
</section>

<section>
  <h2>Analysis Approach</h2>
  
  <p style="color: var(--gray); margin-bottom: 2rem;">Each entry follows a structured format:</p>
  
  <div class="grid grid-3">
    <div class="card">
      <h3 style="font-size: 1.1rem; color: var(--primary);">1. What Happened</h3>
      <p style="color: var(--gray); line-height: 1.8;">
        Objective description of the development. Key facts, dates, parties involved. No interpretation at this stage.
      </p>
      <p style="margin-top: 1rem; font-size: 0.9rem; color: var(--gray); font-style: italic;">
        Standard: Could be verified by anyone reading the source material.
      </p>
    </div>
    
    <div class="card">
      <h3 style="font-size: 1.1rem; color: var(--primary);">2. Why It Matters</h3>
      <p style="color: var(--gray); line-height: 1.8;">
        Impact on businesses, practitioners, policymakers. Compliance obligations or strategic considerations. Audience-specific implications.
      </p>
      <p style="margin-top: 1rem; font-size: 0.9rem; color: var(--gray); font-style: italic;">
        Standard: Actionable insights a practitioner can use immediately.
      </p>
    </div>
    
    <div class="card">
      <h3 style="font-size: 1.1rem; color: var(--primary);">3. Context</h3>
      <p style="color: var(--gray); line-height: 1.8;">
        How this fits into ongoing trends. Connections to other developments. Historical precedents. What to watch next.
      </p>
      <p style="margin-top: 1rem; font-size: 0.9rem; color: var(--gray); font-style: italic;">
        Standard: Helps readers understand significance beyond immediate facts.
      </p>
    </div>
  </div>
  
  <div class="card" style="margin-top: 2rem;">
    <h3 style="font-size: 1.1rem; color: var(--primary);">4. Weekly Commentary (Synthesis)</h3>
    <p style="color: var(--gray); line-height: 1.8;">
      Thematic analysis connecting multiple developments. Trend identification across jurisdictions. Strategic implications for different stakeholders.
    </p>
    <p style="margin-top: 1rem; font-size: 0.9rem; color: var(--gray); font-style: italic;">
      Standard: Adds value beyond individual entries through pattern recognition.
    </p>
  </div>
</section>

<section>
  <h2>Quality Standards</h2>
  
  <div class="grid grid-2">
    <div class="card">
      <h3 style="font-size: 1.2rem; color: var(--primary);">Source Verification</h3>
      <ul style="color: var(--gray); line-height: 1.8; margin-left: 1.2rem;">
        <li><strong>Primary sources preferred:</strong> Government websites, official court documents, regulatory announcements</li>
        <li><strong>Secondary sources vetted:</strong> Established legal publishers, reputable news outlets</li>
        <li><strong>Social media used cautiously:</strong> Only for breaking news, always verified</li>
        <li><strong>No anonymous sources:</strong> All claims must be publicly attributable</li>
      </ul>
    </div>
    
    <div class="card">
      <h3 style="font-size: 1.2rem; color: var(--primary);">Fact-Checking</h3>
      <ul style="color: var(--gray); line-height: 1.8; margin-left: 1.2rem;">
        <li>Cross-reference claims across multiple sources</li>
        <li>Verify dates, jurisdiction, parties involved</li>
        <li>Check for corrections or updates</li>
        <li>Note when facts are disputed or unclear</li>
      </ul>
    </div>
    
    <div class="card">
      <h3 style="font-size: 1.2rem; color: var(--primary);">Conflict of Interest</h3>
      <ul style="color: var(--gray); line-height: 1.8; margin-left: 1.2rem;">
        <li>No undisclosed commercial relationships</li>
        <li>No sponsored content (tracker accepts no advertising)</li>
        <li>Legal analysis reflects professional judgment, not client interests</li>
        <li>Disclose when covering topics where I have professional involvement</li>
      </ul>
    </div>
    
    <div class="card">
      <h3 style="font-size: 1.2rem; color: var(--primary);">Timeliness</h3>
      <ul style="color: var(--gray); line-height: 1.8; margin-left: 1.2rem;">
        <li><strong>Breaking News:</strong> Updated within hours if material with immediate compliance impact</li>
        <li><strong>Standard Updates:</strong> Published next business day at 9:00 AM IST</li>
        <li><strong>Corrections:</strong> Updated immediately with note explaining what changed</li>
      </ul>
    </div>
  </div>
</section>

<section>
  <h2>Limitations & Disclaimers</h2>
  
  <div class="info-box" style="background: #fef3c7; border-left-color: #f59e0b;">
    <h4 style="color: #92400e;">What This Tracker Is Not</h4>
    <ul style="color: var(--gray);">
      <li><strong>Not legal advice:</strong> Analysis is educational and informational</li>
      <li><strong>Not comprehensive:</strong> Covers significant developments only</li>
      <li><strong>Not real-time:</strong> 24-hour lag for most developments</li>
      <li><strong>Not global:</strong> English-language sources only. Heavy focus on US, EU, UK, India</li>
    </ul>
  </div>
  
  <div class="card" style="margin-top: 2rem;">
    <h3>Known Biases</h3>
    <p style="color: var(--gray); line-height: 1.8; margin-bottom: 1rem;">These biases are acknowledged and where possible, I seek out diverse sources to balance them:</p>
    <ul style="color: var(--gray); line-height: 1.8; margin-left: 1.2rem;">
      <li><strong>Geographic:</strong> Stronger coverage of US and EU due to source availability and language</li>
      <li><strong>Sectoral:</strong> Focus on AI and data protection over other tech law areas (by design)</li>
      <li><strong>Commercial:</strong> More coverage of B2B/enterprise implications than consumer issues</li>
      <li><strong>Practitioner lens:</strong> Analysis weighted toward compliance and risk management</li>
    </ul>
  </div>
</section>
