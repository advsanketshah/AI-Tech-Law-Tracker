# Sources Monitored

[← Back to Home](index.html)

This tracker monitors 30+ authoritative sources daily. Only significant developments make it into the tracker based on strict selection criteria.

---

## Selection Criteria

**Included:**
- New legislation, regulations, or regulatory guidance
- Court decisions setting precedent
- Major enforcement actions
- Significant corporate policy changes
- Research with policy implications

**Excluded:**
- Commentary-only articles (no new developments)
- Duplicate coverage of same story
- Marketing content disguised as analysis
- Speculation without factual basis
